Refund Policy

At SHEGLOW, customer satisfaction is our top priority. This Refund Policy outlines the conditions under which refunds may be issued for products purchased through our website.

Eligibility for Refunds

Refunds are available under the following conditions:

  • The product must be returned in its original packaging, unused, and in the same condition as when it was received.
  • Refund requests must be made within 30 days of the purchase date.
  • Proof of purchase, such as a receipt or order confirmation, must be provided.

Non-Refundable Items

Certain items are not eligible for refunds, including:

  • Gift cards or promotional items.
  • Items marked as "final sale" or "non-returnable" at the time of purchase.
  • Products that have been opened, used, or tampered with.

How to Request a Refund

If you meet the eligibility criteria and wish to request a refund, please follow these steps:

  • Contact our customer support team at the email address provided below.
  • Include your order number, proof of purchase, and a brief explanation of the reason for the refund request.
  • Our team will review your request and provide instructions for returning the product, if applicable.

Request a Return or Exchange

If you want to return or exchange a product, please enter your email address below and click "Submit". Our team will contact you shortly.
