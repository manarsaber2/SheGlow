Shipping Policy

At SHEGLOW, we strive to provide fast and reliable shipping to our customers. This Shipping Policy outlines the shipping options, costs, and delivery times for products purchased through our website.

Shipping Methods

We offer the following shipping methods:

  • Standard Shipping: Orders are processed and shipped within 2 business days. Delivery time is 5-7 business days.
  • Express Shipping: Orders are processed and shipped within 2 business days. Delivery time is 2-3 business days.
  • Overnight Shipping: Orders are processed and shipped within 2 business days. Delivery time is 1 business day.

Shipping Costs

The cost of shipping is a flat rate of $50. This applies to all shipping methods, and the fee will be added to your order total at checkout.

Order Processing Time

All orders are processed within 2 business days. Orders placed on weekends or holidays will be processed the next business day.

International Shipping

We currently offer international shipping to select countries. Shipping fees and delivery times for international orders will vary depending on the destination.

Tracking Your Order

Once your order has shipped, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can use this number to track your order on the carrier's website.

Damaged or Lost Packages

If your package is damaged or lost during transit, please contact our customer support team for assistance. We will work with the carrier to resolve the issue.
